design & inspiration

HARMONY OF TASTE AND FORM

The restaurant is a space where haute cuisine meets architectural aesthetics. Each dish is the result of meticulous attention to detail, a careful selection of premium ingredients, and inspiration drawn from modernism and brutalism. The interior embodies a blend of natural textures, clean geometric lines, and refined simplicity. The restaurant’s owner believes that food is not just about taste, but a form of art that deserves to be served in an elegant and thoughtful environment.


As the designer of the kitchen management interface, I was likewise inspired by brutalism, minimalism, and modernism. These styles shaped my vision of a clear, structured, and highly functional platform. The minimalist approach allowed me to strip away the unnecessary and focus entirely on efficiency, while brutalist aesthetics brought a sense of boldness, control, and presence. These principles became the foundation of my design — a system built to support the kitchen in working harmoniously, cleanly, and with creative precision.

project description

MINIMALISM IN MOTION

This project is a restaurant kitchen management interface designed with a focus on clarity and usability. Inspired by modernism, brutalism, and minimalism, it allows chefs and kitchen staff to easily manage dishes, assign cooks, and update menu items in a structured and elegant environment. The platform ensures intuitive control over the kitchen workflow, keeping the design clean, functional, and aesthetically aligned with the restaurant’s concept.


KEY FEATURES

DISH MANAGEMENT - easily view, update, and delete dishes, including details such as name, price, description.

DISH TYPE MANAGEMENT - categorize dishes by type making it easier to manage and filter menu items.

COOK ASSIGMENT - assign and remove cooks from specific dishes to ensure proper staffing and smooth kitchen workflow.

CHEF ACCESS - grant chefs exclusive access to update dish details, manage the kitchen team, and oversee kitchen operations.

REAL-TIME UPDATES - monitor and update dish status and assignments instantly, ensuring efficient communication and workflow in the kitchen.
